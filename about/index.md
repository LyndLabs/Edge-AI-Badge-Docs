# The Cutting Edge
**Edge AI doesn't have to live in the cloud when it can live on you!**

The badge runs inference directly on a $3 microcontroller, and we want to work with hardware vendors like you to bring new demos and capabilities to life!

Custom expansion plates, and plug & play modules unlock functionality like [security at the edge](), and makes your badge compatible at upcoming events!
